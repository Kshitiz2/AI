Chinese President Xi Jinping will take part in President Joe Bidens climate summit this week, the government announced Wednesday.Xi will participate in the online event by video link from Beijing and deliver an important speech, the official Xinhua News Agency said.China and the United States are the biggest emitters of climatechanging carbon pollution.Xi said in December that Chinas emissions would fall by 65 from 2005 levels by 2030.Wednesdays announcement gave no indication whether Xi might make additional commitments at this weeks meeting.

Prime Minister PM KP Sharma Oli has called an allparty meeting for Saturday.PMs press advisor Surya Thapa told Setopati that the meeting will be held in Baluwatar at 11 in the morning. He added that all the parties represented in the federal parliament have been invited for discussion on contemporary politics, COVID19 pandemic and other issues. Infection is surging in Nepal in recent days while the Oli government also looks under threat with CPN Maoist Center threatening to withdraw its support for the government.

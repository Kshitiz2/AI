President Bidya Devi Bhandari has inaugurated distribution of Melamchi water in the Kathmandu Valley on Friday.President Bhandari has formally inaugurated distribution of Melamchi Water Supply Project opening a tap at Bhrikuti Mandap amidst a program in the afternoon. A few taps have been put at Bhrikuti Mandap in a way that water coming from Melamchi is distributed round the clock for public consumption.Distribution of Melamchi water at some places in Kathmandu and Lalitpur districts had started a few days back.
